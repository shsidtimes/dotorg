    COLORS of SHSID: Club Showcase

    Cover Story • Reported by Aaron Xue • Included in March-April 2025

    As spring awakens, SHSID’s truest colors come to life, revealing our school as a diverse and unique garden in full bloom. One way to get a glimpse into the dazzling world of student life is to explore the vibrant mosaic pieced together by our clubs, where our diversity and creativity shine brightest. From anime to dance, and from racing to math modeling, our students have it all. Beyond the classroom, we each have burning passions that drive us— whether in science, the arts, speech- making, or beyond.
